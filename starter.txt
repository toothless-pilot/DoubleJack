We wanted to build this project because we wanted to develop a game that not only tested our coding abilities but also our logical thinking skills. This game tests us not just in python but in math, as we need to decide what to do in a game with much more nuance than the original blackjack game

Our game is called Doublejack, and is a variant of Blackjack. This game essentially gives players a second life if they bust, or go above 21 points. The variation of this game is that if a player busts, he can choose to double down, doubling their bet size and playing a second game to 42.

The dealer draws cards until their total is greater than or equal to 36, and if they bust in this second round, the player wins the entire game. This gives the player a second chance to win, but the odds of winning the doublejack is lower, but the reward is greater.
