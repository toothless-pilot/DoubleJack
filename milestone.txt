Our game, doublejack, is a variant of blackjack intended that further increases the reward
compared to the standard game. The game is built around the dealer hitting the number 21, 
which can be incredibly frustrating to players with a good hand. Our game compensates for
this moment by allowing players to play doublejack, where players can place new bets and continue
playing their previous hands but this time up to the number 42 (instead of the standard 21).
Since the bust threshold is doubled, the payout is also doubled (although losses are still 1:1!).
This gives the player an opportunity to make their money back after a tough beat to the dealer.

Our game can be played either in multipler or singleplayer. In either case, the startGame()
function will ask the player relevant questions like name and budget. In multiplayer you play
against other people while in singleplayer you play against an AI that follows the basic strategy
and adjust its bet according to the True Count. For our game, Dealer stands on 17. The player 
can continue to play rounds until they run out of budget.
